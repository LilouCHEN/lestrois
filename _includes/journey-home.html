The Journey

It all started a decade ago with a small wine management company in Bordeaux. It was a challenging journey but it opened doors and led us to gaining deep insight on the French wineindustry. The experience was priceless.

Along the journey, we continued to grow using our extensive research and knowledge inviticulture from grasping the concept of terroir to the finer details of the vinification process.

We are blessed to have continuing deep mutual relationship with our skilled wine masters with shared values that taught us every wine has its worth and presence. And it is not measured by its price tag.

With all above, we learned to adapt, change, and improve over the years and nowto revolutionise.

At Les Trois, we believe excellent wines are exclusively crafted while retainingits unique heritage and character.
